Margherita Pizza

Recipe for the true Napolitanian Pizza

I will show you how to prepare the Vera pizza Napolitana, la Margherita!

Ingredients

List od ingredients for 4 little pizzas

  • 500gr Flour
  • 190ml Water
  • 14gr Salt
  • 2gr Fresh Yeast
  • 500gr Mozzarela Fior di latte
  • 250ml Tomato Sauce
  • Basilico
  • 2 Mushrooms (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Put 190ml on a Bowl
  2. Add Yeast and disolve
  3. Add 250gr of Flour and knead
  4. Add 14gr of Salt and knead
  5. Knead and let rest covered for 1 - 2 hours
  6. Make 4 dough buns and put them in a covered container
  7. Let him rest for 24-48 hours in the fridge
  8. Take it out of the fridge 2 or 3 hours before cooking
  9. Roll out the dough and add the sauce and cheese
  10. Put in the Oven for 3-4 minutes ar 400CÂ°
  11. Remove from oven and add fresh basil leaves
  12. Enjoy the true Pizza!
